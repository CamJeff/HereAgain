Funny Persian Words/Expressions

A list of some funny, memorable, or offensive phrases that I should keep in mind. In fact, many of these words were the first things I said in Persian!

Curse words

  • Farsi
    کُسِ نَنَت
    Pronunciation
    koseh nanat
    Meaning
    Probably the most offensive Persian curse word. 'Kos' means pussy, and 'nanat' is a colloquial term for mother, so it roughly translates to 'Mother's pussy' or 'Motherfucker'. If you say this someone, you'll probably get into a fist fight.
  • Farsi
    کِیرِ خَر
    Pronunciation
    kireh khar
    Meaning
    'Kir' means dick, and 'khar' mean donkey, so it means 'donkey dick'. It's typical to utter this to yourself while you're roadraging or when somebody nearby in public is being loud and annoying.
  • Farsi
    گُه خُردَم
    Pronunciation
    goh khordam
    Meaning
    'Goh' means shit, and 'khordam' means I ate, so it translate to 'I ate shit'. This is typically used as a profuse apology/grovelling.
  • Farsi
    کُس مَغز
    Pronunciation
    kos maghz
    Meaning
    'Kos' means pussy, and 'maghz' means brain, so this means 'pussy head/brain'.
  • Farsi
    کُس کِش
    Pronunciation
    kos kesh
    Meaning
    'Kos' means pussy, and 'kesh' is a stretchy elastic band, so I guess this means 'pussy stretcher'? That seems like a compliment to me, but I guess it's offensive.
  • Farsi
    کُس خُل
    Pronunciation
    kos khol
    Meaning
    We know what 'kos' means, and 'khol' is describing something foolish. Taken together, this phrase is just an insult/name-call similar to 'idiot'.
  • Farsi
    کُس میخ
    Pronunciation
    kos meekh
    Meaning
    Another 'kos'/'pussy' curse word that means 'idiot'. 'Meekh' translates to nail, as in a hammer/nail.
  • Farsi
    کُس‌ لیس
    Pronunciation
    kos lees
    Meaning
    We know what 'kos' means, but 'lees' means 'lick', so this translates to 'pussy licker'. Again, not very insulting if you ask me. Some may take this name as a badge of honour.
  • Farsi
    خَرکُس
    Pronunciation
    khar kos
    Meaning
    Again, 'khar' is donkey, 'kos' is pussy, so this mean 'donkey pussy' literally, but is used in the same spirit as calling someone an idiot.
  • Farsi
    کَلِه کیری
    Pronunciation
    kaleh kiri
    Meaning
    'Kir' means dick, and 'kaleh' means head, so this phrase means 'dickhead'.
  • Farsi
    کیرَم تو دَهَنِت
    Pronunciation
    kiram tō dahanet
    Meaning
    'Kiram' is my dick, 'tō' is you/your, and 'dahanet' is your mouth, so it means 'my dick in your mouth'.
  • Farsi
    ریدَم دَهَنِت
    Pronunciation
    reedam dahanet
    Meaning
    We know what 'dahanet' means from above, and 'reedam' means 'I shat', so this classy phrase translates to 'I shat in your mouth'.
  • Farsi
    سوسمارخور
    Pronunciation
    soosmār khor
    Meaning
    'soosmār' means lizard, and 'khor' refers to eating, so this means 'lizard eater', and is used as a slur against Arabs.
  • Farsi
    مَلَخ‌ خور
    Pronunciation
    malakh khor
    Meaning
    Similar to the last one, 'malakh' means locust, and 'khor' refers to eating, so this means 'locust eater', and is used as a slur against Arabs.
  • Farsi
    کاکا سِیاه
    Pronunciation
    kākā see-āh
    Meaning
    'see-āh' means black, and this slur is essentially the Persian equivalent of the n-word.
  • Farsi
    کونی
    Pronunciation
    koonee
    Meaning
    'koon' means ass, and a 'y' sound at the end, means that the word is related to that noun. So literally, 'koonee' just means related to ass, but it is in fact used as a homophobic slur, equivalent to the f-slur.
  • Farsi
    ضَعيفِه
    Pronunciation
    za-eefeh
    Meaning
    'za-eef' means weak, and so this term means 'weak person', but it used as a sexist slur against women, calling them 'weak'.
  • Farsi
    خایِه‌مال
    Pronunciation
    khai-eh māl
    Meaning
    'khai-eh' is a vulgar word for testicle, and 'mal' means someone who rubs, so this literally means 'ball/testicle rubber', and is used to describe someone who sucks up to people in power, similar to the term 'kiss-ass' or 'brown noser'.
  • Farsi
    مادَر جِندِه
    Pronunciation
    mādar jendeh
    Meaning
    'mādar' means mother, and 'jendeh' is a curse word that is sort of the equivalent of 'bitch', so this phrase means 'son of a bitch'.
  • Farsi
    حَروم‌زادِه
    Pronunciation
    haroom zādeh
    Meaning
    'Haroom' is the colloquial way of saying 'harām' or forbidden, and 'zādeh' means offspring of, so this really just means 'bastard'.
  • Farsi
    پِدَر سَگ
    Pronunciation
    pedar sag
    Meaning
    'Pedar" means father, and 'sag' means dog, so I guess this means 'dog father', but it's really a light insult that is somewhat equivalent to calling someone an 'idiot' or a 'jerk'.

Expressions

  • Farsi
    تو کُونِش عَروسِه
    Pronunciation
    tō koonesh arooseh
    Meaning
    Literally translates to 'A bride/wedding in your ass', which means that the person is very excited/happy.
  • Farsi
    چِه خَریه
    Pronunciation
    cheh khareyeh
    Meaning
    Literally means 'what a donkey', and I've been told you can use this when something is very cute.
  • Farsi
    دودول طَلا
    Pronunciation
    doodool talā
    Meaning
    'doodool' is a childish way of saying penis, similar to the word 'willie' or 'peepee' in English. 'Talā' means gold, so this means 'golden penis', and is used by mothers as an endearing phrase to describe their young sons. Little creeped out by this one.
  • Farsi
    خاک بَر سَرِت
    Pronunciation
    khāk bar saret
    Meaning
    Literally means 'dirt on your head' (khāk=dirt, bar=on, saret=your head). Initially I thoght it meant you hope the person dies/gets buried, but upon further research, I found that during the mourning period, it was customary for Iranians to pour dirt over their own heads as a sign of grief. So this could, in fact, mean that you are wishing death upon a loved one of the person you say this to. Also, this insult is very versatile, you can say it to your mortal enemy, or to close friend in a playful way.
  • Farsi
    گوز به ریشِت
    Pronunciation
    gooz beh reeshet
    Meaning
    Literally means 'fart on your beard' (gooz=fart, beh=on, reeshet=your beard), which I guess is a creative way of expressing your distate of someone.
  • Farsi
    ریدَم رو روحِ مادَرِت
    Pronunciation
    reedam roo rooheh mādaret
    Meaning
    Literally means 'I shat on your mother's ghost' (ridam=I shat, roo=on, roohe=ghost/spirit, mādaret=your mom). This is quite an offensive insult that not only insults the person you're saying it to, but their entire bloodline.
  • Farsi
    نَه اینوَری نَه اونوَری، کیرَم تو بِیتِ رَهبَری
    Pronunciation
    nah eenvari na oonvari, kiram tō beiteh rahbari
    Meaning
    Means 'neither this way nor that way, my dick in the Leader's office'. A nice little rhymey chant that I've seen/heard from the recent Iranian protests in response to the Ayatollah's tyrannical governance over Iran, especially in response to the regime's extremely brutal crackdown/murdering of protestors. This phrase basically means 'fuck the Ayatollah/Khamenei'.
  • Farsi
    موش بُخورَدِت
    Pronunciation
    moosh bokhoradet
    Meaning
    Literally means 'may a mouse eat you' (moosh=mouse, bokhoradet=may it eat you), and means 'you are cute', typically used for children.

    Pronunciation

    Meaning
